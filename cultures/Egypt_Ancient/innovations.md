# Innovations of Ancient Egypt

## Technology
- Hieroglyphic writing system
- Papyrus production
- Advanced stone-working and construction techniques
- Irrigation systems for agriculture
- Development of the calendar (solar and lunar)
- Medical practices and surgical techniques
- Shipbuilding and navigation

## Art
- Monumental architecture (pyramids, temples, obelisks)
- Realistic and idealistic sculpture
- Wall paintings with distinctive style
- Jewelry making with precious metals and stones
- Pottery with characteristic designs

## Science
- Mathematics (geometry for pyramid construction)
- Astronomy (calendar systems, star charts)
- Medical knowledge (mummification techniques, surgical procedures)
- Chemical processes (glass making, metalworking)

## Architecture
- Pyramid construction techniques
- Temple architecture with columns
- Monumental stone construction
- Sophisticated engineering for large-scale projects
- Urban planning and drainage systems

## Administration
- Bureaucratic record-keeping systems
- Tax collection methods
- Standardized weights and measures
- Legal codes and judicial procedures

## Agriculture
- Flood management systems for the Nile
- Crop rotation and farming techniques
- Animal husbandry practices
- Food storage and preservation methods
