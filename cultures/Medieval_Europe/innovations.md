# Innovations of Medieval Europe

## Architecture
- Gothic architecture (pointed arch, ribbed vault, flying buttress)
- Cathedral construction techniques and engineering
- Castle and fortification design
- Urban planning in medieval towns
- Stone masonry techniques

## Technology
- Heavy plow for northern European agriculture
- Three-field crop rotation system
- Windmills and watermills for mechanical power
- Mechanical clocks and timekeeping
- Horse collar and horseshoe for transportation
- Shipbuilding improvements (cog ships)

## Education
- University system establishment (Bologna, Paris, Oxford, etc.)
- Scholastic method of learning and debate
- Cathedral schools and monastic education
- Preservation and translation of classical texts
- Academic disciplines and degrees

## Law
- Canon law system
- Common law development (especially in England)
- Legal procedures and court systems
- Trial by jury innovations
- Legal education and profession

## Agriculture
- Heavy plow for clay soils
- Three-field crop rotation
- Horse collar innovations for plowing
- New crops and improved farming techniques
- Water management and irrigation improvements

## Commerce
- Banking systems and credit
- Money-changing and money-lending
- Merchant guilds and trade networks
- Bills of exchange and commercial law
- Market regulations and urban economic systems

## Military Technology
- Castle and fortification design
- Siege warfare techniques and equipment
- Heavy cavalry and mounted warfare
- Crossbow and other projectile weapons
- Military organization and tactics

## Literature and Arts
- Vernacular literature (epics, romances)
- Gregorian chant and polyphonic music
- Illuminated manuscripts
- Gothic sculpture and stained glass
- Architectural innovations

## Science and Medicine
- Scholastic method of inquiry
- Integration of Aristotelian philosophy
- Medical schools and hospital systems
- Anatomical studies (limited)
- Astronomical observations

## Mechanical Inventions
- Mechanical clocks
- Windmills and watermills
- Improved agricultural tools
- Various metalworking techniques
- Textile production improvements
