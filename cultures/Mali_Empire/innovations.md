# Innovations of Mali Empire

## Education and Scholarship
- University of Timbuktu and Sankore University
- Islamic legal and theological education
- Manuscript production and preservation
- Libraries and centers of learning
- Integration of Islamic and traditional African knowledge

## Trade and Commerce
- Sophisticated gold dust currency system
- Complex weighing and quality control systems
- Long-distance trade organization
- Credit systems for merchants
- Banking and commercial practices

## Urban Planning
- Development of major trade cities (Timbuktu, Gao, Niani)
- Urban infrastructure for trade and scholarship
- Mosque and palace construction
- Water management in desert cities
- Market organization and regulation

## Political Organization
- Manden Charter as constitutional framework
- Integration of diverse peoples under unified rule
- Provincial administration system
- Vassal relationship management
- Diplomatic protocols and practices

## Literature and Oral Tradition
- Preservation of oral histories through griots
- Epic literature (Sundiata Epic)
- Historical documentation and chronicles
- Musical and poetic traditions
- Genealogical preservation

## Agriculture
- Irrigation systems in river valleys
- Advanced farming techniques for West African climate
- Crop diversification and management
- Livestock breeding and herding techniques

## Architecture
- Mosque construction techniques adapted to local materials
- Palace and royal compound design
- Fortification systems for major cities
- Building techniques for Sahel environment

## Metallurgy
- Gold mining and processing techniques
- Iron working and tool production
- Jewelry and decorative metalwork
- Weapon and tool manufacturing

## Law and Governance
- Integration of Islamic law with traditional practices
- Administrative systems for large empire
- Judicial procedures and legal codes
- Tax collection and revenue systems

## Cultural Synthesis
- Integration of Islamic and traditional African practices
- Religious tolerance and syncretism
- Multicultural administration
- Cross-cultural exchange facilitation
