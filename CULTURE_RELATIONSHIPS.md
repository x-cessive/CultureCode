## Influence Patterns

### Western Tradition
- Egyptian → Greek → Roman → Medieval European → Renaissance → Modern Western
- Roman law and governance influenced legal systems worldwide

### Islamic World
- Greek → Persian → Islamic Golden Age → Ottoman → Modern Middle East
- Islamic scholars preserved and expanded Greek knowledge, passing it to Medieval Europe

### East Asian Tradition
- Chinese philosophies and institutions influenced Korea, Japan, and Vietnam
- Confucianism and bureaucratic systems spread throughout East Asia

### African Civilizations
- Ghana Empire → Mali Empire → Songhai Empire → Modern West Africa
- Trans-Saharan trade networks connected North and sub-Saharan Africa

### Mesoamerican Continuity
- Olmec → Maya → (Aztec/Toltec) → Modern Indigenous Peoples
- Mathematical and astronomical knowledge developed independently
