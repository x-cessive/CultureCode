## Cultural Innovation Hubs

The following cultures served as major centers of innovation:

1. **Alexandria/Egypt** (Hellenistic period): Mathematical and scientific center
2. **Baghdad** (Islamic Golden Age): Center of translation and scientific advancement
3. **Timbuktu** (Mali Empire): Center of Islamic learning in Africa
4. **Cordoba** (Islamic Iberia): Center of learning and tolerance
5. **Athens** (Classical Greece): Birthplace of democracy and philosophy
6. **Rome**: Legal system and administrative innovation
7. **Various Chinese dynasties**: Technological and governmental innovations
